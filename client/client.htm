Chattrr

Chattrr is a new way to talk on the web. Just install the bookmarklet by dragging it to your toolbar, then whenever you go to a website, click it and talk to the other people who are also browsing it.

Drag this link to your toolbar: Chattrr

It's great for blogs that have turned off commenting or websites that you have questions about and want an answer from the herd!

It's been tested on Firefox and Chromium. It won't work on Internet Explorer!
